Here the admin adds the gadget to the main database. All of the gadgets that the admin added
will be shown at the devicePost.html page. Here, the admin can upload specifications of the device,
a photo, and relevant information about the device from his perspective (personal thoughts). Later, using the information
on the devicePost.html page, users start forum threads. Though, device posts and forum are not directly related,
posts on the forum are meant to be about the uploaded gadgets.
